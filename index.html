About Me

I’m an algorithm development engineering intern at Edwards Lifesciences, working in the domain of data analytics for healthcare. My technical background entails medical signal/ image processing, data analysis/ statistical inference & medical device development. As an engineer by training, I am passionate about prognosticating, coming up with preventive actions, improving the existing conditions of & solving persisting problems plaguing our society, specifically in the health sector. One of my long-term goals is to mine data to make a difference in building goal-specific, affordable products that are accessible by everyone. In addition to these, I enjoy critical reading, writing & hiking. I also have a penchant for understanding how the brain works.
